Halim bananor recipe

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
daal 2 bati
meat 500 gm
salt 2 spoon
spice 1 spoon

Step 1: First put daal & meat together. Then add salt & spice & cook for 30 mins. Here you go! Halim!!
